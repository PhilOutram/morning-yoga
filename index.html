How it works

A gentle 5-minute morning routine targeting the lower back and legs — the areas most likely to feel stiff after sleep.

During a stretch

  • A countdown timer shows how long to hold each pose
  • Three soft pips warn you in the last 3 seconds
  • Each new stretch is announced aloud — tap the speaker icon to mute
  • Use the arrows to skip forward or go back
  • Tap Pause to freeze the timer at any time

Tips

  • Move slowly and breathe deeply throughout
  • Never force a stretch — gentle discomfort is fine, pain is not
  • For the forward folds, bend your knees as much as you need to
  • Add the pose images into the poses/ folder to see illustrations
